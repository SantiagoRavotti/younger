Mike Younger - Founder CEO

After suffering an accident while skiing in the Austrian Alps during his winter holiday and not receiving adequate coverage from his contracted insurance company, Mike Younger decided to found Younger Insurance Corporation in 2013. With his two partners, Andrew Garcia and Denali Anand, they managed to grow their small start-up company into a market leader. Mike has a degree in Business from the Madrid University and has been in the insurance industry for the last 10 years.
